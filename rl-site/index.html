Borderless Lab 365 is a web-based remote laboratory platform developed by AP, PolyU. By registering the desired experiment on this website, users are able to real-timely and remotely control the setup residing in PolyU.

This project is sponsored by Quality Education Fund (QEF) of Education Bureau. All services provided on this website are free of charge to local schools.

Working Principle

Through the PolyU server, the remote experiment setups residing in PolyU receive commands from users and make responses. The users can in return monitor the experiment via live cameras, as well as retrieve data obtained by the sensors.

AWARD

The RemoteLab project was awarded the "Asia Gold Award" in the Wharton-QS Stars Reimagine Education 2016 Competition, for creating a novel, true-to-life remote laboratory to enhance student understanding of experimental methodologies and outcomes. The RemoteLab is the first-of-its-kind in Asia, and has enabled students to conduct experiments everywhere at all times.
